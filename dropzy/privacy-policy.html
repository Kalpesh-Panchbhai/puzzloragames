Privacy Policy

Dropzy by Puzzlora Games — Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

Puzzlora Games ("we", "us", "our") built Dropzy as a free-to-play mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Automatically collected:

  • Device type, operating system version, and device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Game progress, scores, and gameplay statistics
  • Crash logs and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • General analytics events (via Firebase Analytics)

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other sensitive device data

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To display ads (via Google AdMob)
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To understand gameplay patterns and improve game balance
  • To sync game progress if you use cloud save

Third-Party Services

Dropzy uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — analytics and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management

Advertising

We show ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Data Retention

Game data stored locally on your device persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Cloud save data in Firebase Firestore is retained as long as your account exists. Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Dropzy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
